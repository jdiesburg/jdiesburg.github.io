Board of Directors
The day to day operations of the PF HOA are run by an all-volunteer Board of Directors, along with help from many other committee volunteers. The Board establishes the annual dues assessment and is responsible for using the money assessed to maintain and improve HOA property and o er programs which are of value to Prairie Fields residents, thereby increasing the value of the neighborhood.

The BOD generally meets at 8:00 PM on the second Monday of each month in the library at Carrie Busey School. See the calendar for current scheduling. The Board may also meet at other times as deemed necessary by the President, and conducts business via email, too. All email items are discussed at the regular monthly board meeting, so that the meeting minutes re ect emailed action. Agendas, minutes, and other related documents from the BOD meetings are available on the Meetings page.



What are the o ces of the Board of Directors?
In general, the HOA Board of Directors will have the o ces of President, Vice President, Secretary, Treasurer, and Member at Large. The o cers are usually elected at the rst Board meeting by the Board following the annual association meeting. The term of o ce for a Board member typically lasts one year. The duties for each o ce are:

President
Presides at all HOA Board meetings
Makes sure orders and resolutions are carried out
Signs contracts

Vice-President
Takes the place of the President when he/she is absent
Performs other duties as required

Secretary
Keeps meeting minutes
Records votes

Treasurer
Receives and deposits monies
Signs checks
Prepares the annual budget

Member at Large
Participate in Board voting and discussions
Performs duties as assigned
Serve on Architectural Control Committee
