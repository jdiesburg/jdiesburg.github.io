COVENANTS

ABOUT COVENANTS

Covenants are legal documents that lay out the guidelines for the planned community.

The Covenants are recorded in the county records in the county where the property is located and are legally binding. This means that when you purchase a lot or a home in Prairie Fields you automatically become a member of the HOA. The Covenants, which were established by the builder, are the rules of the Prairie Fields neighborhood. They govern what you can, cannot, or must do with respect to your home.

How are covenants enforced?

At the 9/20/10 Board of Directors meeting, the Board o cially approved a Covenant Enforcement Policy. The purpose of the policy is to clarify the way in which the Board plans to enforce cove- nants, and to clarify the procedures for enforcement of the covenants. At this time, only complaints which are brought to the Board and which comply with the policy will be acted on.
